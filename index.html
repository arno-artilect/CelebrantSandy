Welcome, I'm Sandy

Your story, beautifully told

Hello and welcome – I'm an International Celebrant with roots in New Zealand, now bringing world-class ceremony expertise to South Africa. My background spans both hemispheres, giving me a unique perspective on crafting ceremonies that honor diverse traditions, cultures, and love stories.

Trained and qualified in New Zealand — a country known for its high standards in celebrancy — I bring that same level of professionalism, warmth, and meticulous attention to detail to every ceremony I create here in South Africa. Whether you're tying the knot, welcoming a new life, or renewing your vows, you deserve a celebrant who understands that every detail matters.

Life is full of emotion, and I believe ceremonies should reflect the real you — with heart, with honesty, and with all the little details that make your story unique. Let's create a ceremony that feels just right — professional, personal, and perfectly you.
